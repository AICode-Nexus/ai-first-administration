# Administrative Request Checklists

Use the closest checklist. If a request spans multiple types, combine only the relevant items.

## Asset Transfer

Required information:

- Applicant and department
- Current asset user and target asset user
- Asset name, asset number, and current location
- Transfer reason
- Target department or cost center
- Expected completion date
- Evidence or approval from both parties when required

Common risks:

- Asset number missing or inconsistent
- Current owner not confirmed
- Target cost center missing
- Transfer reason too vague
- Timing conflicts with resignation, onboarding, or inventory cycle

Human confirmation:

- Asset status in the asset system
- Whether transfer is allowed under current policy
- Final approval path

## Computer Application

Required information:

- Applicant, department, role, and work need
- Device type or configuration requirement
- New hire, replacement, special project, or exception reason
- Budget or cost center
- Expected delivery date
- Manager or department approval basis

Common risks:

- Business need not clear
- Configuration exceeds standard without reason
- Cost center missing
- Expected date unrealistic
- Duplicate request not ruled out

Human confirmation:

- Inventory availability
- Budget availability
- Device standard and exception approval

## Device Retirement Or Buyback

Required information:

- Applicant, department, and current user
- Asset number, device name/model, and purchase or activation date
- Retirement or buyback reason
- Device condition and evidence
- Inspection result or required inspection arrangement
- Cost center or accounting handling if needed
- Expected processing date

Common risks:

- Asset number missing
- Purchase date or age not provided
- Inspection evidence missing
- Buyback price or policy basis not provided
- Device status not verified in asset system
- Data wipe or information security step not mentioned

Human confirmation:

- Asset status and ownership in the asset system
- Retirement/buyback eligibility under policy
- Valuation or buyback price
- Data wipe and security compliance
- Final approval decision

## Cost Center Update

Required information:

- Applicant and department
- Object to update: employee, asset, expense, supplier, or project
- Current cost center and target cost center
- Effective date
- Reason for change
- Supporting approval or organization change basis

Common risks:

- Current/target cost center missing
- Effective date ambiguous
- Scope of affected assets or expenses unclear
- Retroactive update not explained

Human confirmation:

- Correct cost center code
- Financial or HR approval requirements
- System update completion

## Canteen Or Meal Settlement

Required information:

- Settlement period
- Employee or department scope
- Meal count or consumption data source
- Subsidy or deduction rule
- Cost center or allocation basis
- Exception records and handling logic

Common risks:

- Duplicate or missing records
- Department attribution unclear
- Exception handling not documented
- Formula or allocation logic not stated
- Sensitive personal data exposure

Human confirmation:

- Source data accuracy
- Final settlement amount
- Finance approval and posting
